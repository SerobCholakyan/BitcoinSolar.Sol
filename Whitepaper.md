
BitcoinSolar (BTCSOL) — Whitepaper

A Clean‑Energy Inspired Digital Asset for a Decentralized Future

---

1. Introduction

BitcoinSolar (BTCSOL) is a decentralized ERC‑20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain.
Its mission is to merge the principles of clean‑energy economics with blockchain incentives, creating a token ecosystem that is transparent, sustainable, and community‑driven.

The project draws inspiration from the scarcity model of Bitcoin and the renewable‑energy ethos of solar technology — combining both into a digital asset designed for long‑term value and utility.

---

2. Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a decentralized token ecosystem that rewards participation, encourages sustainable digital mining, and supports a global community of builders and holders.

Mission

• Provide a fixed‑supply, transparent ERC‑20 token
• Build a miner‑friendly ecosystem with desktop tools
• Offer a clean, intuitive dashboard for tracking rewards
• Enable community‑driven growth through open‑source development
• Promote energy‑efficient digital mining models
